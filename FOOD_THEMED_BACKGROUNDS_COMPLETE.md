# Food-Themed Backgrounds Applied to All Pages 🍽️

## Summary
All pages in the TasteBuds app now have inviting, warm food-themed backgrounds! The color palette consists of creamy, peachy, honey, and vanilla tones that evoke the warmth and comfort of delicious food.

## New Background Drawables Created

### 1. **food_background_soft.xml** 
   - **Colors**: Warm cream to soft peach gradient (#FFF8F0 → #FFEDD5 → #FEE2C5)
   - **Used for**: Content-heavy pages like Add Recipe, Recipe Detail, and Blue Fragment
   - **Theme**: Soft, inviting, perfect for reading and viewing recipe details

### 2. **food_background_vanilla.xml**
   - **Colors**: Vanilla cream gradient with caramel tint (#FFFBF5 → #FFF4E6 → #FFEFD5)
   - **Used for**: Profile/Manage User page
   - **Theme**: Light, elegant, personal touch like vanilla ice cream

### 3. **food_background_honey.xml**
   - **Colors**: Honey-butter gradient (#FFF9E9 → #FFEFD0 → #FFE4B8)
   - **Used for**: Feed, My Recipes, Recipes List, Home Host, and Main Activity
   - **Theme**: Warm golden tones like honey drizzled on toast

### 4. **dialog_background.xml**
   - **Colors**: Warm creamy gradient (#FFFEF9 → #FFF5E6)
   - **Used for**: Dialogs (Change Password, Add Ingredient)
   - **Theme**: Subtle, rounded corners for modern dialog appearance

## Color Philosophy

All backgrounds follow a cohesive food-inspired palette:

- **Cream & Vanilla** (#FFF5E6, #FFFBF5) - Light, elegant base
- **Peachy Tones** (#FFE8CC, #FFEDD5) - Warm, inviting middle tones
- **Honey & Butter** (#FFE4B8, #FFEFD5) - Rich, golden accents
- **Caramel Hints** (10-20% opacity overlays) - Depth and warmth

These gradients create a warm, appetizing atmosphere throughout the app that makes users feel welcomed and inspired to explore recipes!
